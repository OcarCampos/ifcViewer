Simple IFC Viewer

IFC Viewer is a lightweight, web-based IFC 3D model viewer that allows you to view and interact with IFC (Industry Foundation Classes) models directly in your browser. Built with That Open Engine components, it provides an easy and seamless viewer for construction and BIM professionals.
